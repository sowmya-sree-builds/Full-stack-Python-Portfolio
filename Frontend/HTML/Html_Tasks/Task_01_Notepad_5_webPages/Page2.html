My Hobbies

One of my favorite hobbies is practicing yoga in my free time.

Yoga has four main types: Power, Hatha, Yin, and Vinyasa.

I enjoy Power Yoga the most, as it improves flexibility and arm strength.

Another hobby of mine is journaling. I like to write down my thoughts whenever I feel cluttered or overwhelmed.

I also enjoy listening to music during my free time, as well as tuning in to technology and awareness podcasts.
