Delicious Paneer Butter Masala

This is one of my favorite Indian dishes. It is creamy, spicy, and goes perfectly with roti or rice.

Ingredients:

  • 200g Paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 2 Onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • Cashew Paste
  • Spices – Turmeric, Chili Powder, Garam Masala, Salt

Steps to Prepare:

  1. Heat butter in a pan and sauté onions till golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1 minute.
  3. Mix in tomato puree and cashew paste cook until the oil separates.
  4. Add spices and stir well.
  5. Add paneer cubes and simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with roti or rice
