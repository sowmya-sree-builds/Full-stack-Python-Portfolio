DECIMAL(S,P)	--- will decide the number of digits we want after a point
CHAR(SIZE)	--- fixed size -- max (0 - 255)
VARCHAR(SIZE)	--- will remove the unnecessary space after allocating the required -- max (0 - 255) if exceeds we use nvarchar,nchar...
