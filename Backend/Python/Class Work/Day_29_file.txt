⚡ The 77 Hours Under Rubble
In February 2023, after the devastating earthquake in Turkey, rescuers pulled out a 13-year-old boy named Kadir alive after being trapped under collapsed concrete for 77 hours.
- The quake: A massive 7.8 magnitude earthquake struck southern Turkey and Syria, flattening thousands of buildings.
- The survival: Kadir was buried under twisted steel and broken walls, with no food or water. His cries were faint, but rescuers refused to give up.
- The rescue: Teams drilled through debris, carefully avoiding further collapse. When they finally reached him, the boy was dehydrated but conscious.
- The aftermath: His survival became a symbol of hope amid tragedy, showing the resilience of human spirit and the determination of rescue workers.

That moment—when a weak voice was heard from beneath the rubble and turned into a miraculous rescue—was as thrilling and heart-stopping as any movie scene, but it was entirely real.
